# Aronson Law Design Bible

## Brand Personality

Aronson Law is sophisticated, confident, understated, and strategic.

The website should feel more like a premium architecture or investment firm than a traditional law firm.

Avoid clichés.
Avoid bravado.
Avoid visual noise.

Confidence comes from restraint.

---

## Mission

Provide thoughtful, strategic legal representation with exceptional preparation, clear communication, and unwavering professionalism.

---

## Core Values

• Preparation over theatrics
• Precision over volume
• Integrity over marketing
• Relationships over transactions
• Sophisticated advocacy

---

## Voice & Tone

Confident.

Measured.

Professional.

Calm.

Never boastful.

Never sensational.

Never use fear-based marketing.
